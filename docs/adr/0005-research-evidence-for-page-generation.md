# Research Evidence is a required but non-blocking page generation capability

PPT generation requires Anna Search as an app capability so Research Planning, Research Collection, and Research Curation can be part of the normal generation workflow rather than an optional enhancement. Research Planning creates a deck-level Research Plan, while Research Collection and Research Curation run per Page Generation Unit; Raw Research Material is retained for auditability but is not grounding evidence until curated into Research Evidence. If search, fetch, image selection, or curation produces a Research Evidence Gap, Page Generation continues and unsupported concrete details must be omitted, generalized, or marked as TBD / 待补充 instead of blocking the deck.

This deliberately rejects making Search Executa optional, letting Page Authoring directly search on demand, folding search execution into Page Plan, or treating raw search results as grounding evidence. Visual Research Evidence must be selected through image inspection comparable to the existing upload-and-analyze visual review flow, because image search metadata alone is not enough to decide whether an asset fits a page.
